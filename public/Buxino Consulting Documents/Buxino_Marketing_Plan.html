3. Feasibility: Why This? Why Now? (2026 Context)

1. Mandatory B-BBEE ESD Spend

Corporates in automotive, mining, FMCG and TMT are legally required to allocate 2–3% of NPAT to Enterprise & Supplier Development. They cannot avoid this spend. Level 1 B-BBEE entities like Buxino deliver 135% procurement recognition — making us the most efficient channel for compliant, high-value expenditure.

2. Return of the Experience Economy

Digital fatigue has reversed the trend toward cheap, mass-produced swag. Executives now value fewer, better, tactile items that reinforce brand prestige. Premium leather journals, engraved metalware, and curated unboxing moments are in demand — exactly what Buxino delivers.

3. Operational Leverage via SETA

By deploying five SETA-funded learners, Buxino maintains high output with dangerously low fixed costs. This model allows us to scale volume and margin faster than competitors burdened by full payroll — now critical to overcome audited 2026 YTD cash constraints.
