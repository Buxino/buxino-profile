Executive Summary

Buxino Consulting (Pty) Ltd is a Level 1 B-BBEE, 100% black-owned strategic services firm founded in October 2021 by Bakang Matjila. We specialize in corporate gifting & branding, retainer-based consulting, strategic business plans, and premium event logistics — targeting blue-chip clients in TMT (25%), FMCG/logistics (25%), automotive (30%), and mining (20%). Buxino operates at the nexus of TMT connectivity and FMCG logistics, delivering Institutional Excellence through strategic branding and execution.

Our Superior Logic model leverages two structural advantages:

  • SETA Force Multiplier: 5–10 merSETA-funded learners handle execution at near-zero direct labor cost, allowing senior leadership to focus on closing high-value retainers.
  • Subscription Retainer Architecture: Quarterly "Season Concepts" convert transactional spend into recurring revenue, targeting R200,000+ lifetime value per anchor client (revised for conservatism).
